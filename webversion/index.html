      Help & XP Guide

      How to Use Chnain

      This guide explains the main study flow, where progress is tracked, and how XP is earned.

      1. Start here

      • Create a semester, then add your subjects in the Planner.
      • Open each subject to add chapters, resources, and an exam date if you have one.
      • Use the Planner as your map of what is left to study and practice.

      2. Daily study flow

      • Go to Pomodoro, choose a subject, and follow the suggested next chapter when available.
      • Start a focus session, then let the app move you into a break when the timer ends.
      • After each session, come back and mark lesson or exercise progress so your planner stays accurate.

      3. Track progress

      • Subjects with exercises are only fully completed when both the lesson and exercises are marked done.
      • Course-only subjects are completed when the lesson part is done.
      • Use Analytics for totals and trends, and Leaderboard to compare sessions and XP.

      4. How XP works

      Important: Select a subject before starting focus. Sessions without a subject do not award XP.
      • Focus sessions give 2 XP per study minute.
      • Completing a chapter gives 20 XP when the subject has exercises, or 10 XP for a course-only subject.
      • Reaching the daily goal of 3 sessions gives a one-time 30 XP bonus that day.
      • Your first study session of the day can also trigger a streak bonus: 15 XP per extra streak day, up to 90 XP.
      • Each new level needs more XP. Level 1 to Level 2 needs 600 XP, and the requirement increases by 140 XP each level.

      5. Offline and sync

      • You can study offline. Your data is stored on this device even without an account.
      • Sign in from Settings if you want backup and sync across devices.
      • Use Sync Status and Sync Now in Settings to check whether cloud changes are uploaded.
